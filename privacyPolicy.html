Privacy Policy


Last updated: 31 October 2025


At Mechanical Keyboards, we respect your privacy and follow the Privacy Act 2020 (NZ).


1. Information We Collect


We only collect the personal information necessary to provide our services:


  • Contact details: name, email, phone number (if provided).
  • Shopping cart information: items selected, quantity, and prices.

2. Why We Collect It


Your information is used to:


  • Process and complete your orders.
  • Contact you about your order or inquiries.
  • Improve our website and services.

We do not collect more information than is necessary.


3. How We Protect Your Information


We take reasonable steps to keep your information safe from misuse, loss, or unauthorised access.


4. Sharing Information


We do not sell or trade your information. We may share your data with trusted third parties only to:


  • Process payments.
  • Deliver your orders.

These third parties must also comply with the Privacy Act 2020.


5. Your Rights


Under the Privacy Act 2020, you can:


  • Access your personal information.
  • Request corrections if your information is inaccurate or incomplete.

6. Contact Us


For questions or requests, submit a contact form on our contact us page.
